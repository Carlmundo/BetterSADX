Settings in config.ini:
DisableSA1TitleScreen: disables the SA1 title screen and main menu background while keeping all other features of DC Branding
RippleEffect: enables the water ripple effect on the SA1 title screen
DrawOverlay: draws a highlight overlay on top of the SA1 logo like in the International release. At 640x480 works only when the ripple effect is enabled.
DisableFade: disables the title screen fade-in effect
EnableTransition: enable the zoom effect on the logo when Start is pressed. Works only when the ripple effect is enabled. The transition itself is not adjustable.
RemoveCream: disable Cream cameos

For resolutions above 640x480 only:
BackgroundOffsetX: offsets the title screen/main menu background horizontally (adjusted from top left corner)
BackgroundOffsetY: offsets the title screen/main menu background vertically (adjusted from top left corner)
BackgroundScaleX: scales the title screen/main menu background horizontally
BackgroundScaleY: scales the title screen/main menu background vertically

LogoOffsetX: offsets the SA1 logo horizontally (adjusted from top left corner)
LogoOffsetY: offsets the SA1 logo vertically (adjusted from top left corner)
LogoScaleX: scales the SA1 logo horizontally
LogoScaleY: scales the SA1 logo vertically

CopyrightOffsetX: offsets the SEGA copyright text horizontally (adjusted from center)
CopyrightOffsetY: offsets the SEGA copyright text vertically (adjusted from default position)

SonicTeamOffsetX: offsets the Sonic Team logo horizontally (adjusted from center)
SonicTeamOffsetY: offsets the Sonic Team logo vertically (adjusted from default position)

PressStartOffsetX: offsets the PressStartButton text horizontally (adjusted from center)
PressStartOffsetY: offsets the PressStartButton text vertically (adjusted from default position)

Note that the offsets are automatically scaled depending on current resolution. The logo and the copyright text have an extra Y offset of -48 pixels at 1:5 (1920x1200) and -96 pixels at 21:9 and wider, and the PressStartButton text's original Y position is hardcoded for different aspect ratios.

The offset settings are only editable for 800x600 and higher.

Scaling and offsets are hardcoded for 640x480 and partially hardcoded for ultrawide resolutions (21:9).

Textures:
AVA_GTITLE0_ES.PVMX contains the following textures: PressStartButton, SA1 logo, Sonic Team logo, SA1 logo overlay, copyright text (_small versions are for 640x480)
AVA_TITLE_CMNS.PVMX contains the static title screen background 
AVA_TITLE_CMNX.PVMX contains the animated title screen background 
AVA_TITLE_CMN_SMALLS.PVMX contains title screen backgrounds for 640x480
AVA_TITLE_BACK_ES.PVMX contains the main menu background

Title screen and main menu backgrounds are composed of 4 512x512 blocks and additional 4 256x256 blocks added on the right. Any 1280x1024 image can be split into these blocks using the following script for ImageMagick:

convert title_bg.png -crop 512x512+0+0 title_bg_00.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 512x512+512+0 title_bg_01.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 512x512+0+512 title_bg_02.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 512x512+512+512 title_bg_03.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 256x256+1024+0 title_bg_04.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 256x256+1024+256 title_bg_05.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 256x256+1024+512 title_bg_06.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 256x256+1024+768 title_bg_07.png

For 640x480, use the following script with any 640x480 image:

convert title_bg.png -crop 256x256+0+0 title_bg_00.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 256x256+256+0 title_bg_01.png
convert title_bg.png -background "#cfcfcf" -crop 256x256+0+256 -extent 256x256 title_bg_02.png
convert title_bg.png -background "#cfcfcf" -crop 256x256+256+256 -extent 256x256 title_bg_03.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 128x128+512+0 title_bg_04.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 128x128+512+128 title_bg_05.png
convert title_bg.png -crop 128x128+512+256 title_bg_06.png
convert title_bg.png -background "#cfcfcf" -crop 128x128+512+384 -extent 128x128 title_bg_07.png
